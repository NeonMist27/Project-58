My Favorite Hobby

Dancing

My hobby is dancing. I really like to dance and I feel like it is a part of me. It is easy for me and I do it in my free time. Sometimes I preform and show my dance to audiences. Usually the audience really likes the dance, and they always enjoy the preformance. I learned how to dance last year during the pandemic when I was bored at home sitting in my room. Soon after that I became a very good dancer. Two of my dances are the best ones and I preformed them for birthdays. Although I stopped dancing for a little while because of school, I like to just move around the house while dancing because it gives me motivation to do better and excersise. Sometimes, I preform at school for my classmates and friends, they also very much enjoy watching me dance. What helps me with dancing is my ability to move very quick and light, which I learned from doing Karate. When I do Karate, I am always moving and am usually doing something that resembles dance to me.
